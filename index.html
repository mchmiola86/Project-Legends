For student logins, the userid is your student number. The password is generated based on the following (in order):

1. First letter of your FIRST NAME

2. First two letters of your LAST NAME

3. The two digit number for the day of your birth (If you are born Feb 4, 2000, then it would be 04)

4. Last 3 digits of your student number

So John Smith with student number 90294431, born on March 23, 2001 would have a password of: JSM23431
